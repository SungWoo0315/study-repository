       ↳	android.view.View

     	 	   ↳	android.widget.AbsSeekBar
     	 	 	   ↳	android.widget.RatingBar

    - ex) [LinearLayout](https://developer.android.com/reference/android/widget/LinearLayout)  

        java.lang.Object
